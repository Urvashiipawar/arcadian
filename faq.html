Frequently Asked Questions

What is Arcadian?

+

Arcadian is a university project designed to showcase a curated collection of games hosted from various external websites. It serves as a platform to explore different gaming experiences and demonstrate web development skills.

Who created Arcadian?

+

Arcadian was created by a student as part of a university project, driven by a passion for video games and web design.

Are the games on Arcadian developed by you?

+

No, the games featured on Arcadian are hosted from other websites. We are not the developers of these games. We simply provide links or embed them for your convenience and enjoyment.

How do I play the games?

+

Each game on Arcadian is linked to its original hosting site. Simply click on the game you're interested in, and you'll be directed to the external site where you can play it directly.

Is Arcadian free to use?

+

Yes, Arcadian is a free project. Any costs associated with playing games on the external sites (e.g., in-game purchases or premium access) are determined by those respective platforms, not by Arcadian.
