ABOUT US

Arcadian is a university project dedicated to celebrating the joy and diversity of video games. Created by a passionate student gamer, this platform serves as a curated showcase of fantastic games hosted from various sources across the web.

Purpose: The Evolution of the Arcade

As a university project, Arcadian aims to explore web development and design principles while providing a fun and accessible space for users to discover and enjoy a variety of games. We've brought together a collection of engaging games, hand-picked for their entertainment value and unique gameplay.
